Frequently Asked Questions

What software do you use for 3D modeling?
I primarily use Blender for 3D modeling, along with additional tools for texturing and optimization. Blender is excellent for game asset creation due to its powerful modeling tools and free availability.
How long does it take to create a custom 3D model?
The timeline varies depending on complexity. A simple low-poly prop might take 2-4 hours, while a detailed character with rigging could take 20-40 hours. I always provide detailed timelines during project planning.
Do you work with specific game engines?
Yes! I specialize in Godot and Unity, but I can create assets for any game engine. I ensure all assets are properly optimized and include necessary files for easy integration.
What file formats do you provide?
I provide multiple formats including .fbx, .obj, .blend, and engine-specific formats. All assets come with proper UV maps, textures, and documentation for easy implementation.
Can you create stylized art in different styles?
Absolutely! I specialize in stylized low-poly art but can adapt to various styles including cartoonish, realistic, and fantasy aesthetics. I always discuss style requirements during project planning.
Do you offer revisions and support?
Yes, I include revision rounds in all projects and provide ongoing support for asset integration. I want to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.
